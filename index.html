Visit a Meteorite

Instructions
Type the name of a city, address, state or country to see nearby meteorites!

After searching you will see multiple pins on the map showing where meteorites have fallen! The pins will display information about the meteorite such as how much it weighed, what year it fell, and meteorite name.

Powered using the NASA meteorite landing API and Google Maps API.

Successful Search!
Meteorites nearby will populate on the map with a green pin, if you click on the pin it will display information about that specific meteorite.

In order to return to the home page, you may click on the title of this page.
